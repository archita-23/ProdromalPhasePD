The Research Gap

Bridging the divide between subtle behavioral changes and clinical diagnosis.

What is Missing?

Most existing systems are supervised, meaning they require labeled data (diagnosed vs. healthy). However, in the prodromal phase, patients are technically "healthy" but show slight deviations.

The gap lies in longitudinal multi-signal analysis: analyzing how sleep, mood, and fatigue interact over time rather than looking at them individually.

OUR INNOVATION

Using unsupervised anomaly detection, our system identifies "personalized deviations" from a user's own baseline, rather than comparing them to a general population average.

TECHNICAL EDGE

Our hybrid model processes multi-domain data (sleep, mood, activity) to find correlated "risk clusters" that signal neurodegenerative onset.
